# SRE Arena

> The open arena for evaluating AI-native operational systems.

SRE Arena is a community initiative focused on creating realistic, reproducible, and transparent evaluation approaches for modern operational intelligence systems.

As AI-native tooling rapidly enters operations, incident response, observability, and infrastructure workflows, the ecosystem lacks a shared way to evaluate how these systems behave under real operational conditions.

Today, most evaluations happen through isolated demos, private benchmarks, or synthetic workflows that fail to capture the ambiguity and complexity of real-world operations.

SRE Arena exists to change that.
---

## What We Care About

Operational systems are different.

They operate in environments that are dynamic, noisy, distributed, and partially observable. Evaluating them requires more than static tasks or scripted workflows.

We believe operational intelligence should be evaluated through:
- realistic runtime scenarios
- transparent methodologies
- reproducible environments
- safety-aware reasoning
- community-driven evaluation

Not benchmark theater.

---

## Current Direction

SRE Arena is currently exploring:
- incident investigation evaluation
- operational reasoning under uncertainty
- runtime context handling
- remediation safety
- observability-driven workflows
- multi-system operational scenarios

The initiative is intentionally infrastructure-agnostic and aims to evolve collaboratively with the broader ecosystem.
